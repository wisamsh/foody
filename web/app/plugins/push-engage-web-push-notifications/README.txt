PushEngage is a plugin to enable Browser Push Notifications for Desktop and Mobile users on Chrome, and Firefox, Opera, Edge, Safari, UC Browser, Samsung Internet Browser. You can implement in 10 mins.

== Description ==

[PushEngage](https://pushengage.com) offers you an easy to use Web Push Notification plugin for your website. We support all browsers - Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Microsoft Edge, Safari, UC Browser, Samsung Internet on both desktop and mobile devices.

Now re-engage with customers with targeted push notifications. Within a few minutes, you can install and go live with Push Notification on your website.

Once visitor subscribers to your push notification, you can auto-update visitors about your new post. The visitor will receive your push notification even when they are not on your website. You can even segment your subscribers based on their interest and re-engage them with relevant push notifications.

PushEngage offers free as well as paid plan. You can also choose 14 days of free trial for any of the paid plans before purchase. PushEngage also has features designed specifically to increase the Engagement Rate and Conversion Rate for your push notification campaigns.

= Features =

* **Multiple Browser Support:** PushEngage Currently Supports Push Notifications on Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Microsoft Edge, Safari on both desktop and mobile devices.

* **HTTPS and HTTP Support:** PushEngage supports both HTTP and HTTPS websites.

* **Customize Opt-In:** You can choose from 7 different types of Subscription Opt-In. You can even customize your opt-in with your messaging.

* **Segmentation:** Segment your subscribers based on different attributes like browser, geo, interest, and other parameters. For e.g., if the user has subscribed from Firefox browser, you can segment them as Firefox push subscribers. Send targeted notifications to each segment for better engagement.

* **Drip Campaign:** Nurture your subscribers and build your brand by using Drip Push Notification campaign. Send a series of automated notifications whenever a new visitor subscribes. This way you can keep your subscribers engaged to your website and increase repeat visits.

* **AB Testing:** Do AB testing for your push notification campaign. You can test multiple parameters of push notification and see which one works best for your subscribers.

* **Auto Push:** You can set Auto Push on for your post. Whenever a new article is published, the platform will send push notification to subscribers automatically.

* **Triggered Campaign:** Create an auto-trigger campaign based on customer action. If you are offering free e-book and customer has downloaded, you can trigger a specific push notification to be sent to them automatically.

* **Schedule Your Notifications:** Schedule push notifications so you don’t miss to send notifications during the weekend, or on vacation. Schedule your notifications to be sent in subscriber’s time zone and when they are most active.

* **Analytics:** Optimize your campaigns using the Push Analytics data. Check out how each notification performed. Analyze to see which days, notification, campaigns are giving you best result and accordingly make changes in your push notification strategy.

== Installation ==

1. Install PushEngage from the WordPress.org plugin directory or by uploading the PushEngage plugin folder to your wp-content/plugins directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the “Plugins” menu in WordPress. Follow the instructions to complete the setup.
3. To enable single step opt-in (only HTTPS website), follow the below instructions as mentioned [here](https://blog.pushengage.com/how-to-install-web-push-notifications-plugin-on-wordpress-website/) or watch this Video [youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6_FnUphO80c]
